Michael Jordan

15:15 | Chicago

Michael Jeffrey Jordan (born February,17,1963),also know by his initial MJ, is anAmerican businessman and former professional basketball player. His biography on the official NBA website states:"By acclamation, Michael Jordan is the greatest baskteball player of all time". He played fifteen seasons in the National Basketball Association(NBA), winning six NBA championships with the Chicago Bulls. Jordan is the principal owner and chairman of the Charlotte Hornets of the NBA and of 23XI Racing in the NASCAR Cup Series. He was integral in popularizing the NBA around the world in the 1980s and 1990s, becoming a global cultural icon in the process. Jordan played college baskeball for three seasons under coach Dean Smith with the North Carolina Tar Heels. As a freshman, he was a member of the Tar Heels'national championship team in 1982. Jordan joined the Bulls in 1984 as the third overall draft pick, and quickly emerged as a league star, entertaining crowds with his prolific scoring while gaining a reputation as one of the game's best defensive players.
